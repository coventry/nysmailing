Tell New Yorkers they're about to be disenfranchised!

You can use the form below to generate fliers you can print out and mail to NYS residents who are not registered as Democrat. These fliers inform people of a looming deadline after which many NYS voters will be unable to participate in next year's Democratic Presidential Primary.

Residents of NYS must be registered as Democrat with the Board of Elections by October 9 if they want to participate in the 2016 NYS Presidential Primary.

There is a phone banking effort underway to persuade as many NYS voters as possible to register by October 9. However, the voters for whom the NYS Board of Elections provides a mailing address outnumber the phone banking list by a couple of orders of magnitude. Also, although it's more expensive, sending mail to people is considerably less effort per contact, and probably more likely to reach people.

To use these fliers, print them out with the address sheets on one side and the first page of the registration form on the other.

Put stamps on the two "postage" boxes, and fold them into quarters so that the recipient's address is exposed and the board of elections address is hidden. That means folding vertically first, then horizontally. Seal the letter with a small piece of sticky tape, and you're ready to save a voter from disenfranchisement!
